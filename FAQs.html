Here are some frequently asked questions about how to stay organized and motivated.
Don't forget to manifest your way to success!


DotInk aims to help students stay organized by providing an easily accessible platform for them to compile class schedules, zoom links,
assignments, extracurricular meetings, and many more activities. It also gives them a safe space to plan out their weeks, set goals,
and self reflect on how the day went.

DotInk keeps our students motivated by creating a space where they can listen to the best lofi beats while studying the wonderful secrets
of coding. It makes sure that they don’t get distracted and stay on task as they can follow a pomodoro timer for occasional breaks.

• Work when you have the most amount of energy, for some it is during the morning while for some it is during the night.
• Find a quiet place to work and make sure that your phone is far away or turned on do not disturb.
• Take breaks! Give yourself 5-10 minute breaks away from all screens.
• Listen to music but without lyrics to distract you Don’t be afraid to ask for help from your peers and or teachers.
• Most importantly: just start, you got this!

• Have set work hours and stick to them to avoid being overworked and burnt out.
• Make sure that you have a set workplace so that your brain knows what to associate with work and what to associate with relaxation.
• Make time to exercise. Treating your body right means a fresher mind!
• Prioritize important tasks and split up your work reasonably. Setting unrealistic goals will stress you out more than you need to.
• Again, take breaks. This is super important to re-energize throughout the day.

S – Specific; What do you want to accomplish? (Use the 5 W’s)
M – Measurable; How can you measure your progress?
A – Attainable; Do you have the skills or the drive to complete this goal?
R – Relevant; Why are you setting this goal now?
T – Time-bound; What is your deadline? Is it realistic?
